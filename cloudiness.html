Cloudiness

cloudiness

There are three broad bands where Earth's skies are most likely to be cloudy: a narrow strip near the equator and two wider strips in the mid-latitudes. As warm, moist air converges at lower altitudes near the equator, it rises and cools and therefore can hold less moisture.
